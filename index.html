About

I’m a tech enthusiast with a passion for blending front-end development, programming, and hardware integration. Currently pursuing B.Tech in Electrical & Electronics Engineering (EEE) at GEC Palakkad (KTU), I’m driven by the intersection of software and hardware—leveraging my skills to bridge the gap between code and real-world applications. My journey began with a 2-year Computer Science course under the Kerala Govt Board after matriculation, where I built responsive/non-responsive websites and experimented with converting them into Android apps using online tools. Alongside, I’ve dabbled in C, C++, Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, SciPy), and now I’m expanding my expertise as an aspiring Data Scientist, taking online courses to master analytics and machine learning. What excites me most is the potential of integrating programming with EEE—whether it’s IoT, embedded systems, or data-driven automation. When I’m not debugging circuits or writing code, I’m constantly exploring new technologies, upskilling, and working on side projects that challenge me to grow. Let’s build the future, one line of code (or circuit) at a time!
